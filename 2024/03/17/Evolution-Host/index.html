Locations

Evolution Host boast a wide array of locations around the globe including: Canada, Germany, UK, USA, Poland, Finland, France, India, Asia and Australia. This allows you to achieve the lowest latencies possible and target specific audiences.

Enterprise grade hardware is standard at Evolution Host. With the latest, fastest Intel and AMD CPU’s, DDR4 RAM and RAID10 NVMe SSD Arrays, our services are at the leading edge of technology in the hosting industry.

Privacy is a priority here at Evolution Host. We will never distribute your data (in any form) for monetary gain, your data is not ours to sell. Enjoy the peace of mind that comes with Evolution Host services.
